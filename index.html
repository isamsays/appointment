What is NBI Clearance and Why Do You Need It?

NBI clearance is an official document issued by the National Bureau of Investigation (NBI) that certifies you have no criminal record in the Philippines. It's a must-have for:

  • Job applications (local and abroad)

  • Travel and visa requirements

  • Business permits and transactions

  • Bank transactions and other legal purposes

Requirements for NBI Clearance Online Application

Before you start your online application, prepare the following:

  • A device with internet access (computer, laptop, tablet, or smartphone)

  • A valid and unexpired government-issued ID (e.g., passport, UMID, driver's license, PRC ID)

  • Personal information (full name, birthdate, address, etc.)

  • Active email address and mobile number

  • Payment for the NBI clearance fee

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Apply for NBI Clearance Online

1. Register on the Official NBI Clearance Website

  • Visit the official NBI clearance portal.

  • For new applicants, click "Sign Up" and fill in the required details.

  • If you're renewing, log in with your existing account.

2. Verify Your Account

  • After signing up, you'll receive a One-Time Password (OTP) via SMS or email.

  • Enter the OTP within 5 minutes to verify your account.

3. Complete the Online Application Form

  • Fill out all required fields accurately (personal details, contact information, etc.).

  • Double-check your entries; you can edit or delete information if needed.

4. Upload a Valid Government-Issued ID

  • Scan or take a clear photo of your ID.

  • Upload it as part of your application for identity verification.

5. Schedule Your NBI Clearance Appointment

  • Choose your preferred NBI branch, date, and time for your appointment.

  • Only weekdays (Monday to Friday) are available; weekends are closed.

6. Pay the NBI Clearance Fee

  • Select your payment method: online banking, Bayad Center, 7-Eleven, mobile apps, or over-the-counter bank payment.

  • Keep your payment receipt and reference number as proof.

7. Attend Your Appointment for Biometrics and Photo

  • Bring your payment receipt and valid ID.

  • Appear in person at your chosen NBI branch for photo capture and fingerprinting.

8. Claim Your NBI Clearance

  • If there are no issues ("No Hit"), you can receive your clearance the same day.

  • If there's a "Hit" (possible name match with a criminal record), further verification may take up to two weeks.

What to Bring on Your NBI Clearance Appointment

  • Printed application form (if available)

  • Original and photocopy of your valid ID

  • Payment receipt or proof of payment

  • Any supporting documents (if required)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How long is NBI clearance valid?NBI clearance is valid for 12 months from the date of issuance.

2. Can I reschedule my NBI appointment?Rescheduling is not allowed online. If you miss your appointment, visit the NBI office after 10 days for assistance.

3. What is a "Hit" in NBI clearance?A "Hit" means your name matches someone with a criminal record. Additional verification is required, which may delay the release of your clearance.

4. What if I encounter payment issues?Try another payment method or check your network connection. Ensure all details are correct before resubmitting payment.

5. Can I renew my NBI clearance online?Yes, as long as your previous clearance was issued from 2016 onwards. Log in to your account and follow the renewal steps.

Pro Tips for a Smooth NBI Clearance Application

  • Keep your payment receipt safe until you receive your clearance.

  • For any issues, contact the NBI hotline or email their support.
